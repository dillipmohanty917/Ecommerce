About Us

  • Medleymed Network has been promoted by Medleymed Network Solutions Pvt Ltd, a 100% subsidiary of Athena Global Technologies Limited, a leading Hyderabad based Technology Services Company.
  • Medleymed harnesses the power of technology to orchestrate and simplify the complex Pharmacy value chain. We are improving accessibility, reliability and affordability through efficient demand aggregation, supply chain reengineering and useful analytics.
  • As an innovative start-up led by the young thinkers and doers from top institutes like IIMs and IITs, we operate at the cutting edge of technology and innovation. We have a powerful competitive advantage because of our business model, platform quality and strong implementation team.
  • Our vision is to become the largest B2B technology platform in the Healthcare space in the country seamlessly facilitating the business interactions between Manufacturers, Distributors and Pharmacies through easy Order placements and attractive Deals.

    • Medleymed Network

  • Medleymed Network is enabling thousands of Pharmacies and hundreds of Distributors across India to grow their business, using technology and knowledge.
  • As your dedicated B2B marketplace, we help you minimize costs and maximize profits. Our platform is open 24/7, allowing you to conduct transactions anytime, anywhere, ensuring continuous business operations.
  • Medleymed Network enables Pharmacies the ease of buying all their pharmacy and general items. In short, it is a one stop solution for the retailers to place orders to multiple distributors for their pharmacy needs.
  • Medleymed Network provides real time details on products, offers, schemes, pricing and availability of medicines.Come, be part of a groundbreaking transformation that unites the entire industry on one seamless platform that helps you to minimize your costs and maximize your profits.
